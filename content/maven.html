Qpid via Maven

The following dependencies can be added to the POM for your Maven build to utilise the artifacts via the Maven central repository.

Qpid JMS

Jakarta Messaging 3.1 (jakarta.jms)

<dependency>
  <groupId>org.apache.qpid</groupId>
  <artifactId>qpid-jms-client</artifactId>
  <version>2.1.0</version>
</dependency>

Jakarta Messaging 2.0 (javax.jms)

<dependency>
  <groupId>org.apache.qpid</groupId>
  <artifactId>qpid-jms-client</artifactId>
  <version>1.7.0</version>
</dependency>

Qpid ProtonJ2

Client

<dependency>
  <groupId>org.apache.qpid</groupId>
  <artifactId>protonj2-client</artifactId>
  <version>1.0.0-M11</version>
</dependency>

Engine

<dependency>
  <groupId>org.apache.qpid</groupId>
  <artifactId>protonj2</artifactId>
  <version>1.0.0-M11</version>
</dependency>

Qpid Proton-J

<dependency>
  <groupId>org.apache.qpid</groupId>
  <artifactId>proton-j</artifactId>
  <version>0.34.0</version>
</dependency>

Qpid AMQP 0-x JMS Client

<dependency>
  <groupId>org.apache.qpid</groupId>
  <artifactId>qpid-client</artifactId>
  <version>6.4.0</version>
</dependency>
<dependency>
  <groupId>org.apache.geronimo.specs</groupId>
  <artifactId>geronimo-jms_1.1_spec</artifactId>
  <version>1.0</version>
</dependency>
